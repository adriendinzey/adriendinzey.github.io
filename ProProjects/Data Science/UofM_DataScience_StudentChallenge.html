University of Manitoba Data Science Nexus/Bison Transport Student Challenge

I am currently participating in this challenge hosted by the University of Manitoba Data Science Nexus. Out of the three possibilites, we chose the Bison Transport challenge. The goal of this challenge is to address network imbalances in Bison Transports wide and vast transport route. An imbalance in this scenario is defined to be a whenever inbound freight does not match outbound freight. We must create a model that looks at properties such as timing , geographic and equipment to accurately identify imablances. Stay tuned to see our submission!
