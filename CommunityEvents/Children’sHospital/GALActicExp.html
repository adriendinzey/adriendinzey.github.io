Children’s Hospital Foundation of Manitoba GALActic Experience

The Children's Hospital Foundation of Manitoba hosts a gala to help raise funds, awareness and the spirits of sick and injured children and their families. As part of the entertainment for this evening, my colleague Abram Kremer and I created a Roblox game tailored to the "Galactic" theme. The GALActic Experience was another step up from our last game, while keeping the "challenge completion" gameplay elements alive. This included a blast off into space to begin the game which surprisingly was very difficult to code! The game is still live if you would like to check it out!
