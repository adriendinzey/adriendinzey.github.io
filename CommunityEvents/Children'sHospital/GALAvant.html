Children's Hospital Foundation of Manitoba GALAvant

The Children's Hospital Foundation of Manitoba hosts a gala to help raise funds, awareness and the spirits of sick and injured children and their families. This was our second gala we helped out for, so again my colleague Abram Kremer and I created a Roblox game tailored to this year's "GALAvant" theme. The idea given was that the theme was travelling the world. We thought the best way to give the experience of travelling the world virtually was to create a game where each "level" is a completely different part of the world. We also identified another dimension to this theme, medieval knights. The word "galavant" struck that cord with us and so we created a game where a knight is tasked with collecting Dr. Goodbear teddy bears from around the world to save the day!
