Math Songs

Some History

Since my Sophmore year of highschool I have had the hobby of making "Mathematical Parodies" of popular songs. It began as a bet between myself and my Math teacher. One day we were looking at Math parodies of popular songs, but I didn't enjoy them very much. I mentioned to my teacher that myself and some classmates could make a "Math Song" of much higher quality. The teacher offerred us "an extra 4%". Without asking any specifics we set off and created our smash hit "Parabola Baby". From then on, we made these songs purely out of passion.
